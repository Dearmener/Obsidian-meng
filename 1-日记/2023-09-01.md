## 今日新建
```dataview
table stauts as "状态",
priority as "优先级",
deadLine as "期限" ,
creationDate as "创建日期" 
from "101-任务" 
where dateformat(date(split(creationDate," ")[0]),"yyyy-MM-dd") = dateformat(date(file.name),"yyyy-MM-dd") 
or dateformat(date(deadLine),"yyyy-MM-dd") = dateformat(date(file.name),"yyyy-MM-dd") 
sort priority desc,creationDate desc
```
